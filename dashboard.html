How is the Situation?

Understanding the situation of people with disabilities can make it easier for the NDIS to help them.

Disabled people profile



The first image is a dashboard of people with disabilities in Australia. There are two factors influencing the situation of disabled people in Australia, one is age and the other is gender. There are four sub-graphs in the dashboard, which are a bar chart with the distribution of persons with disabilities in different ages and genders, a bubble chart with age distribution characteristics, a pie chart with gender distribution characteristics, and age distribution characteristics. bar chart.

The following information can be seen intuitively from the figure. First, people with disabilities exist in all age groups and genders. This means that when setting up web pages, businesses need to consider meeting the needs of all users. Second, the number of disabled people increases with age. This means that the elderly need more attention from enterprises, so the type and frequency of services need to be increased with age when setting up rescue activities. Finally, the proportion of disabled men and women is almost the same, so companies should not have any preference for gender.

`

Disabled people needed activities



The second image is a dashboard of types of disability assistance activities. Cognitive or emotional tasks, communication, health care, household chores, meal preparation, mobility, property maintenance, reading or writing tasks, self-care and transport are all assistance activities that people with disabilities need. There are two sub-graphs in the dashboard, one is a word cloud chart of the types of assistance activities that the disabled need, and the other is a chart of the proportion of the types of assistance activities that the disabled need.

The following information can be seen intuitively from the figure. First of all, the relief activities that people with disabilities need are diverse, spanning medical, artistic, and community fields. Secondly, health care, cognitive or emotional tasks, and communication are the three activities with the highest proportion, so they should be paid attention to by enterprises, and enterprises should provide disabled people with priority and a large number of assistance activities related to these three aspects.

`

Disabled people participation

The third graph shows the types of daily activities that people with disabilities need. art or craft or practical hobby group activities, performing arts group activities, religious or spiritual group activities, sport or physical recreation with others, visited relatives or friends, voluntary or community service activities, went on holidays or camping with others, went out with relatives or friends are daily activities required by disabled people.

This graph provides an overview of the needs of people of different ages for different daily activities. The following information can be seen intuitively from the figure. First, every age group has more or less needs for every activity. This means businesses need to target participants of all ages when planning their events. Second, different age groups have inconsistent needs for different activities. Although all three age groups preferred to visit relative or friends, they showed different rankings in each of the same activities.
