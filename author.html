What I want?





I am a highly sensitive person. This sensitivity comes from my father's influence on me. My father was a doctor, and I knew from a young age that he had the power to save many lives. Whenever I listen to him tell the story of his healing, I find that I feel the pain of what I hear. Therefore, from a young age, I was inspired to help more people change their lives just like my father.

When I came to Australia to study my master's degree, a chance volunteer opportunity brought me into contact with the NDIS program. Its goal is to lead vulnerable people to live a normal life. I've come to realize that there is a group that provides individualized support to people with disabilities in its own way. So, I decided to become one of the contributors to this project, hoping to do my part for those in need in the society.

During my time in the NDIS Volunteer Program, I have been thinking about how I can use my strengths to achieve my aspirations. I have more than 6 years of professional background in information systems and more than 1 year of Internet internship experience. Therefore, the best way for me is to build a website for disabled people to help them find the information they need in a timely manner. This is the original intention of this website.

My goal is to make more people aware of the existence of vulnerable people, empathize with them and give them more love through this personalized website. At the same time, I also call on more people who want to change people's lives to understand and join the NDIS project to provide higher quality services for people with disabilities, which is a meaningful and valuable thing.
